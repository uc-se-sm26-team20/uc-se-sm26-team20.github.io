About the Project

Team's 20 is articulating a messenger app that essentially brings people together within their community while still having private messaging capability. It is for a wide range of ages 12+ and meant to be user friendly. The app is designed to help people within a community who are feeling left out to feel engaged and bring upon opportunities.
